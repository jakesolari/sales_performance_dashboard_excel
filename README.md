Interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 10,000+ sales transactions across regions, product categories, and sub-categories using PivotTables and PivotCharts.
# Sales Performance Dashboard — Excel

## Overview
Interactive sales dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using the Tableau Superstore dataset (10,194 transactions, FY2023–2026).

## Visuals
- Monthly Revenue Trend (line chart)
- Sales by Region (bar chart)
- Sales by Category (donut chart)
- Top 10 Sub-Categories by Profit (horizontal bar)
- KPI Scorecard — Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Quantity, Margin

## Key Findings
- West and East regions drive 60%+ of total revenue
- Copiers are the most profitable sub-category at $56K
- Overall profit margin sits at 12.56% across all categories

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel — PivotTables, PivotCharts, data formatting
